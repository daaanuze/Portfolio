MINI SKIRTS FOREVER!

The world is changing, mini skirts are a symbol of female liberation. Let's question traditional standards and usher in a new era of sexual freedom. The new contraceptive pill has been produced and more than 1.2 million American women are using birth control to take control of their lives.

In 1966, when Dior’s fashion show didn’t feature the hottest trend of the moment, a group of women known as the “British Society for the Protection of Mini Skirts,” stood outside the show in protest with signs that read, “Mini skirts forever”.

Women still don't have the same freedom and opportunity as men, that being said, mini skirts are the least of women's problems. However, this moment in fashion history goes to show how important clothing can be as a tool of empowerment, freedom, and expression.

To that, we say, “Mini skirts forever!”
